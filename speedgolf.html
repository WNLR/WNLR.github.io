$50 includes 9 holes of speed golf, lunch, and awards - Proceeds to benefit the Wednesday Night Laundry Runners Scholarship Fund

Come join us for a fun morning of golf + running/fast walking and help raise money for the WNLR Scholarship Fund! 9 holes of the fastest golf you’ll ever play!

We are deeply honored this year to have the legendary Steve Scott joining us as one our participants. Among his many awesome accomplishments, Steve held the American record (3:47.69) in the mile for over 26 year and holds the world record for the number of times of breaking the 4 minutes barrier in the mile (136). Most importantly, of course, Steve is regarded as the founder of Speed Golf! In 1979 Steve played a regulation 18 hole course in under 30 minutes while shooting a respectable 95.

What is speed golf?

Here are the basics:
- Your score is your golf score + your time elapsed between your first shot and your final putt on hole 9.
- Players will tee off singly about 3 minutes apart. If you pass someone, just yell “Fore” and hit away. We will attempt to seed people to minimize passing but still may happen!
- No carts. This is a running/fast walking event. You may carry a maximum of 6 clubs + putter. No limit on how many balls you carry :-)
- You don’t have to rake sand traps or pull pins. This is speed golf!
- You can choose whether to play the “white” tees (normally the men’s tees), “green” tees (for men 70+), or the “red” tees (normally the women’s tees).
