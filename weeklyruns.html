Featured Runs

Famous Wednesday Night Laundry Run

This route was established in 1965 and is how the running club got its name. It has been used as a weekly run for more than 50 years.

    Day of the week and time: Wednesday 5:30 PM ish

    Location: Lucky's parking lot in PG Meet near Dominos

    Typical distance: 6 mile run, but can be shortened to 4 or extended to 8

    Terrain: 90% roads, some trails, one big hill on Congress road, Winter route is all pavement

    Primary contact name: Tom Burns

    Contacts e-mail: montereywnlr@gmail.com

    Usual number of people: 10-20

    Group approximate mile pace for this run: runners of all abilities 6 minutes to 12 minutes

    Strava Routes: 6 mile summer loop, 8 mile Summer loop, 4.5 mile Winter loop

    Additional Comments: First Wednesday of each month is New Runner Wednesday! Of course newcomers are welcome every run.
