Runs are open to everyone even if you are not a member and all locations are walk-friendly. All runs start on time so be sure to arrive a few minutes early.

Featured Runs

Pacfic Grove - Wednesday Night Laundry Run

    Day of the week and time: Wednesday 5:30 PM

    Location: Hops and Fog

    Typical distance: 6-mile run, but can be shortened to 3 or extended to 8

    Terrain: Mix of roads

    Primary contact name: Emily Cole

    Contacts e-mail: info@wnlr.org

    Usual number of people: 10-20

    Group approximate mile pace for this run: runners of all abilities 6 minutes to 12 minutes

    Strava Routes: 6 mile loop

    Additional Comments: First Wednesday of each month is New Runner Wednesday! Of course, newcomers are welcome every run.
