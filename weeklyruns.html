Famous Wednesday Night Laundry Run

This route was established in 1965 and is how the running club got its name. It has been used as a weekly run for more than 50 years.

    Day of the week and time: Wednesday 5:30 PM (during Daylight Savings, March - November)

    Location: Mission Linen Laundry across from Pacific Grove High School track

    Typical distance: 6 mile run, but can be shortened to 4 or extended to 8

    Terrain: 90% roads, some trails, one big hill on Congress road

    Primary contact name: Jim Allen

    Contacts phone number: 831-649-1175

    Contacts e-mail: btlbaum@sbcglobal.net

    Usual number of people: 10-20

    Group approximate mile pace for this run: runners of all abilities 6 minutes to 12 minutes

    Strava Routes: 6 mile loop, 8 mile loop

    Additional Comments: The location of the Wednesday Night run changes to the recreational trail in Monterey (across from NPS) when the time changes in the Fall and it is dark at 5:30. Please contact Jim Allen if you are unsure where to meet
