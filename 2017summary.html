2017 Scholarship Program Summary

Thanks to the generous donations from our members and local businesses, WNLR gave away over $10,000 in scholarship awards to outstanding track and cross country student- athletes in Monterey County’s High School Graduating Class of 2017. To receive this award, eligible student-athletes must have some of the most competitive track and cross country times in Monterey County, must have a GPA of at least 3.0, must demonstrate good character and leadership qualities as described in a letter of recommendation from a coach or teacher, must be actively involved in community service, and may demonstrate financial need.The eligible student-athlete intends to run competitively at the collegiate level.

This year, nine award winners were chosen from a countywide pool of eligible student- athletes. Many of these student-athletes you can find running local races.

Best Overall Female - Evelin Meza, Greenfield High School

For every distance over 800 meters, Evelin is this year’s outstanding female athlete. Her league- and school- record-setting track and cross country times, as well as her academic performance (3.94 GPA), superb traits as a role model, and commitment to her team’s success are nothing short of brilliant, and these combined qualities make her a stellar all-around candidate for this scholarship. As one of the top runners in the entire Central Coast Cross Country Section (CCS), we are proud to recognize Evelin for all of her accomplishments. In addition to exceptional academic and athletic success, Evelin is also a member of Greenfield High Schools Physics Club, Book Club, College Club, and Volleyball Team. Evelin will use her WNLR scholarship to assist with her academic and athletic pursuits at San Jose State in Fall of 2017.
