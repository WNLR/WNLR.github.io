About Us

Our Mission

The Wednesday Night Laundry Runners of Monterey County (WNLR) exists to promote running and educate the public about its benefits. As a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, we strive to foster community, fitness, and fun for runners and walkers of all levels.
