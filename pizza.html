Pizza parties

There are approximately three pizza parties per year where we get together and enjoy pizza provided by the club for members only. These are great times to mix and mingle with other runners in the community with pizza, drinks, a 50/50 raffle, and updates about the club.

Pizza Dates

  • A Wednesday in March at Gianni's pizza
  • A Wednesday in June at Angelina's Pizzeria
  • A Wednesday in October at Mountain Mikes, Marina

Paying members will recieve an email with RSVP information up to two week prior to pizza party.
