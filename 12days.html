The schedule for the 18th annual 12 days of Christmas runnin beginning on Dec. 13th. Please check daily for updates/changes on the 12 days of Christmas Runnin page on Facebook. Make sure to wear your Christmas colors every day and bring your appetite and a FLASHLIGHT. Looking forward to seeing you all out there!!!!

Locations: to learn where the locations are please check the document in 12 days of Christmas Runnin page on Facebook.

Dec. 13 Friday

    Title: Lucky Birthday Christmas Run

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Siara, Tadhg, Clover & Swagger Meehan, Jo & Eleanor Kyler (all 12 and under, and two Birthday Dogs!)

    Description: 2-6-mile run/walk in search of the Lucky Clover on the bike path followed by build your own yogurt parfaits and hot chocolate bar.

Dec. 14 Saturday

    Title: Burnsly Marathon with a side of Spam

    Time: 8:30am

    Host: Tom Burns & Shannon Cronin

    Description: Trail Running in Ft. Ord. We will run and find books hidden on the course and who knows what else....., For the walkers a nice walk in Fort Ord is available followed by Eggs, Spam, and waffles

Dec. 15 Sunday

    Title: Get ZENergized

    Time: 8:30am

    Host: Ben Balester & Yasi Payandehjoo

    Description: Run/walk to General Jim Moore and South Boundary. Serving plant-based sausages & breakfast bites and Egg casseroles and fruit smoothies.

Dec. 16 Monday

    Title: Four Calling Birds

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Jim, Debra, Vera and Alex

    Description: Walk/ run route designed by big dog Jim Allen followed by egg casseroles and bagels to YOUR delight

Dec. 17 Tuesday

    Title: Merry Christmoose

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Liisa Nichols, Tara Clark, & Megan O’neill

    Description: Loop run past the Butterfly Moose & then down along the coast & back up by Lovers Pt to 19th Street. Shorter options available walk/run. Followed by a variety of breakfast type foods: breakfast casseroles, fruit salad, Finnish coffee bread, coffee/tea/hot chocolate/water/juice.

Dec. 18 Wednesday

    Title: OLD SCHOOL 12 days at casa meglenn

    Time: 6:00am sharp!

    Host: Glenn Reis, Lynne & Charlie Denley, Mystere Sapia and special guest chefs

    Description: Run/Walk followed by eggs, meat, and of course potatoes!

Dec. 19 Thursday
