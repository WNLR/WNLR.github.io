The schedule for the 20th annual 12 days of Christmas runnin beginning on Dec. 13th. Please check daily for updates/changes on the 12 days of Christmas Runnin page on Facebook. Make sure to wear your Christmas colors every day and bring your appetite and a FLASHLIGHT. Looking forward to seeing you all out there!!!!

Locations: to learn where the locations are please check the document in 12 days of Christmas Runnin page on Facebook.

Dec. 13 Monday

    Title: Christmas Sox Run

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: The Manzanos

    Description: 5K/10k Run, Walk, Ride followed by Egg casseroles, bagels, juice, and coffee.

Dec. 14 Tuesday

    Title: Hogs and Quiches

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Dino and Karen Pick

    Description: Run/walk/bike to the ocean and around Spyglass Hill followed by quiche, mimosas, and BACON!

Dec. 15 Wednesday

    Title: Sunrise & Surf at Spanish Bay!

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Greg McCormack and Vera Noghera

    Description: 3–5-mile run/walk followed by Bagels, Bites, and Beverages on the Beach Below the Boardwalk.

Dec. 16 Thursday

    Title: No Frills. Drills and Sand Hills

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Mike D’auria and Ben Balester

    Description: Boot camp/run event. Running and obstacle drills on the beach and team relay events on the sand hill. Easy run and walk option along Scenic and/or beach. Coffee, pastries, homemade cookies and fruit/veg smoothies.

Dec. 17 Friday

    Title: Run, then we MANGIA

    Time: 5:30pm

    Host: Carmella Cuva, Glenn Reis

    Description: Sicilian soup will be served with salad and spaghetti after a 3- or 4-mile run/walk

Dec. 18 Saturday

    Title: Tri to conquer some hills!

    Time: 8:00am

    Host: Steve & Beverly Zalan, Maureen Wruck, Linda Sereno, Keely Clifford

    Description: New loop through the scenic Bay Ridge neighborhood to test your legs!  Extra-long route with bonus climbing for the runners to challenge even the speedsters and a few Strava segments to nail :-)breakfast casseroles accompanied by a side of bacon

Dec. 19 Sunday

    Title: Up, Up & Away this Holiday!

    Time: 8:30am

    Host: Marie Earl & Peter Skinner.

    Description: Customizable longer/shorter Run/Walk. Hills (some challenging and some easy peasy) plus neighborhood gems (secret steps, Sloat Monument, Butterfly House, …).  Refuel with homemade blueberry muffins, banana bread, artichoke frittata, wild rice salad with smoked fish.

Dec. 20 Monday

    Title: Monday-Funday-Cinna-Yum-Day

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Emily Cole, Kate Conway, Bobby Hillard, Morgan Ivens-Duran

    Description: Run/Walk followed by cinnamon rolls and other cinnamon-y breakfast delights! Street parking required, Prepare for outdoor eating (tiny apartment), BRING/WEAR LIGHTS!!!

Dec. 21 Tuesday

    Title: Solstice Celebration

    Time: 7:00am

    Host: Tara Clark, Brian Clark, Liisa Nichols

    Description: Winter solstice is at 7:59 am.  We will start at 7 am and run/walk for 59 minutes! This is the shortest day of the year - come in your lightest, brightest sun (and earth) themed gear. Finnish breakfast bread, sunspot skillets, fruit, yogurt, oatmeal.

Dec. 22 Wednesday

    Title: Tour de Parks

    Time: 6:00am

    Host: Siara & Tadhg Meehan

    Description: 2-6-mile run/walk around the Parks of Monterey, followed by Christmas finger foods, coffee and hot chocolate.

Dec. 23 Thursday

    Title: Ribbon Tying Ceremony

    Time: 5:30pm

    Host: Ralph Wege

    Description: Marking the Lake-to-Lake course by tying red ribbons, followed by Pizza, beer and wine

Dec. 24 Friday

    Title: 20th Annual Lake to Lake Holiday Run/Walk

    Time: 8:30am

    Host: Ingrid Aquino

    Description: One hour run/ walk/cycle on rec trail starting and ending at Dennis the Menace Park.Post event festivities TBD
