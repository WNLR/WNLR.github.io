2nd Annual WNLR Track Meet!

Monday, October 16, 5:30 pm
MPC Track

Enough of this marathon/ironman/long-distance stuff.... it's time to show off your track speed! Join the Monday night track crew for an informal meet. Warm-ups start at 5:30, with the first races promptly at 6.

Order of events:

  1. 1600m
  2. 100m
  3. 800m
  4. 200m
  5. 4x400m relay

Please bring a green shirt and a white shirt. We'll divide into teams and have a head-to-head competition. There are sure to be special prizes for our most impressive athletes.

The more the merrier, no matter your speed! You will be able to compete in as many or as few races as you'd like. If you would only like to attend and watch, we would definitely appreciate some volunteers to help start races and record times. Only one month til race day, so start adding in more speed workouts, improve your racing, and let's see how you can do in these shorter events!
